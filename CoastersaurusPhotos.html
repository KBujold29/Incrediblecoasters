Coastersaurus Review

We're here at Legoland California. For you credit whores, there's Coastersaurus. You climb into the cars, and pull down the lap bar. And we're off!!! We start to climb the lifthill. But before we know it, we are rolling down the first drop, which is curved. Wee. You can easily tell that this coaster is not made for adults to ride. We then rise up into an upward helix, which takes all of our speed. Yipee. We meander through a couple turns before turning into some straight track that looks like a brake run, but we're going so slow, anything more would just be stopping us, before turning in the station. Hey, it may be boring, but at least it's not a painful kiddy coasters. But even so, it's still just a tiny kiddy coaster. If you're a credit whore and need the credit, ride it. Otherwise, SKIP!!!!

2/10

Location: Legoland California

Opened: 2004

Built by: Gerstlauter

Last Ridden: December 7, 2012
