Honker Dinger Dash Review

For this review, we're at Sesame Place San Diego, here to review their Helix Mat Racer. These things are a twist on the traditional Mat Racer slide. Half Mat Racer, half regular slide. So go down like a regular slide, only to turn into a racing competition ion the second half. Hmm. I can think we can really expand on this idea. Let's do something truly crazy. But for now, let's just enjoy the standard Helix Mat Racer. We reach the top, and we're off. We give ourselves a good push as we head down a helix drop. You can't see how you're doing compared to the others. But THE FASTER, THE BETTER!!!! Plus, it's more fun anyway to go fast. We then pop out and drop down a couple bumps, turning into a standard Mat Racer. Hopefully we're in first place. And we slide. All in all, this is just a really fun ride that I recommend while at Sesame Place San Diego.

7/10

Location: Sesame Place San Diego

Opened: 2014

Built by: ProSlide

Last Ridden: October 16, 2022
