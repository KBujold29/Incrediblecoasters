We're here at Six Flags Hurricane Harbor, where we'll be reveiwing Tornado. Tornado, as you may have guessed is a Tornado Clone. Ok, time for the review. After climbing up the stairs with your bigass four person tubes, you reach the top, get comfortable in your tube until the lifeguard pushes your tube into the darkness. It feels weird going through the first turn, but rather then going through the usual turn, you drop. It's a fun Tornado Drop, then you go up high in the funnel, only to come back down and do it again. Then rinse and repeat until you reach the end. So yeah, it's a slightly unique Tornado Clone. I recommend riding this if you're at Six Flags New England.

7/10

Location: Six Flags New England

Opened: 2001

Built by: ProSlide

Last Ridden: July 30, 2011
