Cobra Review

All right. We're here at the L.A County Fair, where we're going to review Cobra, the bigger of the two kiddy coasters here. After getting in the surprisingly roomy cars, we're off. We go up a small little spiral lift before heading down a puny little spiral drop. We then dip up a little before heading down a tiny little helix. Its actually semi-fun, providing a lateral or two. Wee. We then head around a turn before we go back in the station and go again. Its just a tiny little kiddy coaster, but its a fun one at that. If you're a credit whore or a little kid, you'll probably have a little bit of fun on this coaster. Other wise, I'd give it a skip.

3/10

Location: American Fairs
