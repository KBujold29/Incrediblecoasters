Little Dipper Review

We're here at Quassy, where we'll be reviewing their kiddy coaster. Little Dipper. If you're really whorish, you'll climb in the car, pull down the lap bar, and away you go. We climb up the lifthill, get up, down a dip, and around a turn. Dip in the turn. Check. Continue around the turn. And now the exciting part. Dip, tiny hill, dip, turn, dip, into the station. YAY!!! You just got a credit. Now go take a shower. Cha-Ching!! =)

2/10

Location: Quassy

Opened: 1952

Built by: Allan Herschel

Last Ridden: July 29, 2011
