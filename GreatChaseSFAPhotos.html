Great Chase Review

We're here at Six Flags America. For you hard core credit whores, there's Roadrunner Express. You squeeze into the cars, and the ride op pulls down the lap bar. It's not very comfortable right now. We start to climb the lifthill. But before we know it, we are rolling down the tiny first drop. You can easily tell that this coaster is not made for adults to ride. We then rise up a little bit and go through a sort of downward helix. This thing has some laterals. And due to how squished we are, that's a bad thing. We go through some tiny bumps and through another uncomfortable turn before heading back in the station. Luckily, I NEVER have to ride this ever again. I would ONLY recommend riding this if you are a HARD CORE Credit Whore.

1/10

Location: Six Flags America

Opened: 1999

Built by: Zamperla

Last Ridden: July 22, 2019
