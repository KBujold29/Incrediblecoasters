Pindsvinet Review

We're here at Fårup Sommerland. For you hard core credit whores, there's Pindsvinet. We squeeze into the cars, and the ride op pulls down the lap bar. We start to climb the lifthill. But before we know it, we are rolling down the tiny first drop. You can easily tell that this coaster is not made for adults to ride. We then rise up a little bit and go through a sort of downward helix. This thing has some laterals. However, this just makes the ride hurt as we get squeezed around. The humps are just uncomfortable, and luckily, it's now over. Yeah. This ride sucks. Even by kiddy coaster standards, it's a rough peice of sh*t. So yeah. It sucks.

1/10

Location: Fårup Sommerland

Opened: 2012

Built by: Zamperla

Last Ridden: June 20, 2014
