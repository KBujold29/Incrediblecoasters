Adventure City Review

Adventure City is a fun little park that is mostly treated as a credit whoring break from a day at Knotts Berry Farm. When most coaster enthusiasts decide to go to the park, all they think about is getting the credits and heading on back over to Knotts to get more rides in on Xcelerator. But Adventure City is really nice. Now of course, the park is meant for small children and its not a place you want to spend all day at, but it was still much nicer than we expected. We were worried at first about getting in, but Adventure City is familiar with the concept of credit whoring and knew what we came there to do. Hell, From what I've heard, they didn't even charge our friends who went a few months ago since they knew that they were just there to get the credits. And even once we were inside, no one looked at us funny or even noticed us. We just got our credits, got a few photos and went on back to Knotts Berry Farm. The staff were very friendly, the theming was pretty good for the type of place it was and even the roller coasters weren't half bad. I mean, nothing here is gonna make any Top 10 Lists, but Rewind Racers is without a doubt, a very fun family coaster. If you're into credit whoring and really want to boost up your count, Adventure City will welcome you to come whore out the coaster there.

Flat Rides

Here are the reviews of all the Flat Rides at Adventure City. Well the star flat ride and the only one that we bothered to do was the Kiddy Drop Tower. Now this isn't a typical frog hopper, but rather its one of those super sized Moser Frog Hoppers that seem to pop up at various different parks such as the Santa Monica Pier. Those are always fun. The rest of the flat rides are kiddy flat rides, but come on. This is a kiddy park. Its expected that they'd have this sort of stuff.

These rides are always fun for a quick ride.

Water Rides

Adventure City currently has no water rides.

Dining

I never ate at Adventure City. Their website says nothing about food, but I'd imagine that you can order typical stuff like hot dogs and hamburgers at the park, stuff you might be able to order at a place like Golf'N'Stuff seems like stuff they'd serve at Adventure City.

Theming and Other Attractions

Here are the reviews of all the other stuff at Adventure City. The theming is just typical childrens park theming. Its not great or anything, but its cute. As for other stuff to do, they have kids shows, rock climbing, a petting zoo, among other fun things for kids to do.

In Conclusion

Adventure City may not be an amazing park or anything, but it is a fun little place. The coasters they have are fun for small kids and decent for credit whoring, the staff are friendly, and its just an overall nice little place. I wouldn't go crazy for this place. But if you're credit whoring in Southern California or have small children, Adventure City is definetly worth an hour of your time.

Enthusiast FAQs.
