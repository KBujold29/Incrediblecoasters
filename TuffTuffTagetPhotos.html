Tuff-Tuff Tåget Review

We're here at Gröna Lund. And since we're credit whoring right now and will get ALL the credits, it's time to roll up our sleeves and get this pathetic little credit. And let me tell you. This one isn't just emberassing, it's REALLY emberrasing. This is one of those kiddy coasters that borders on that fuzzy line of whether it should even be counted as a credit or not, but the Zamperla Mini Mice just barely make the cut of being a credit, and so, here we are. Riding. We hop in the car, and off we go. We go up a tiny lift hill, and from here. Its just a bunch of teeny tiny turns. There's not even a drop. Its just turns. See what I mean when its a borderline kiddy credit? Yeah, that pretty much just sums it up right there. And that's it, though we do get a couple more laps. Joy. So that's the Tuff-Tuff Tåget. It's short, it's humiliating, and you know you're a whore riding this ride.

1/10

Location: Gröna Lund

Opened: 2010

Built by: Zamperla

Last Ridden: June 23, 2014
