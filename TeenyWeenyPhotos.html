Teeny Weeny Review

All right. We're here at Tusenfryd, where we're going to review what is claimed to be the smallest coaster in the world. Teeny Weeny. And honestly, it just might be. This thing is barely even a credit. You just get in the seats (which are surprisingly pretty big for such a small coaster) and we're off. You go up a tiny lifthill (which just might be smaller than you if you're a tall guy) before dropping. Wee. Around a turn and go again and again and again because all the little kids wanna keep riding even though it only took one lap to get the credit, so now you must show off your shame as you keep going around and around. Those Norweigian parents are judging you. *Sigh* Oh well. I got the credit. Cha-Ching.

1/10

Location: Tusenfryd

Opened: 1996

Built by: SBF Vista Group

Last Ridden: June 21, 2014
