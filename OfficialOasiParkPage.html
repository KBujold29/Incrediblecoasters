Oasi Park Review

Oasi Park is a total credit whoring place. It's located in Rome. Just one of many credit whoring spots in Rome. Though this place, like all the Rome Credit Whoring places, is conviniently located in the suburbs of Rome, outside all the tourist attractions. So you'll have to do some walking if you wanna get this credit. So you hard core credit whores will have to get some exercise if you want to get this credit. I know the park liked our prsence as they gave us some postcards so we'd always remember the park. Thanks. But hey, it at least seemed nice by credit whoring park standards.

Flat Rides

Dining

Not only did I did not eat at Oasi Park, but I don't even think they sell food. Maybe there's something, but I honestly wouldn't be surprised if there wasn't anything. Just get some Rome Food, it's really good.

Theming and Other Attractions

Here are the reviews of all the other stuff at Oasi. Well, there's not too much in terms of high quality theming, however, they are bathed in one thing. Illegal Disney Merchandise. And it was fun to just look at all of it. And it's not just Disney, it's everything. I know that Illegal Disney Stuff is pretty much a staple of all crappy credit whoring stops, worldwide. But I think Oasi Park still holds the record. I saw more Disney stuff here than I did at Disneyland Paris. And I honestly don't think there's anything else to this park. It's just kiddy flats and the kiddy coaster. Just go out and explore Rome.

Trust me. There's WAY more than just this.

In Conclusion

Oasi Park is nothing more than a credit whore stop to most coaster enthusiasts. That's all there is here. This is the type of place that makes you look at yourself and say "Why do I have this stupid hobby?". So once you get your credit, just get out and find something cool to do. After all, you are in Rome.
