Comet Review

Today at Waldameer, we'll be reviewing Sea Dragon. This is the park's kiddy woodie. Yeah, it's a kiddy coaster, but this is in all honesty, one of the better ones.After clenching and squishing into the seats, we're off. We roll around a turn and head straight for the tiny little lifthill. While going up the lifthill, we get a nice view of the all the trees. Yeah. We're not above them since it's a kiddy coaster. But it still looks nice. We then reach the top and head down the first drop. The first drop is actually fun and to our surprise, we go up the small hill, we actually get a little airtime. While it's very weak and over quickly, keep in mind that this is a kiddy coaster. After rolling around the turn, we dip back down and get a little more airtime. At this point, you say to yourself, this ride isn't half bad. We then dip down again before rising up into another turnaround. There's not much airtime here, but hey. This is still really fun. Especially for a kiddy coaster. We then dip back down, go through a small little bunny hop, before rising up yet again and turning under the structure. It almost feels like a decent sized woodie. You then dip back down and rise through another tiny bunny hop. Sadly, no airtime here. We then head up into the brake run. It's a really fun kiddy coaster.

4/10

Location: Waldameer

Opened: 1951

Built by: Philadelphia Toboggan Coasters

Last Ridden: June 22, 2021
