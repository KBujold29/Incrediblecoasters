Beach Blanket Banzai Review

We're here at Cowabunga Bay Las Vegas, and we're here to ride the parks family raft ride, Beach Blanket Banzai. Yeah. This is......nothing special. Just an ordinary family raft ride. But hey. Still a fun ride. Let's hop on. After climbing up all the stairs, we get in the raft, and away we go. We go down a helix, gain a little speed, go through another helix in the other direction. Hmm. Gaining a little speed. We go around another helix. This would be a lot better had there been some fatties riding with us. We head around a turn, getting right underneath Wild Surf, before going down a small drop, and splashing into the pool. It's nothing special or crazy, but its a fun slide. And if you're here, you might as well check it out.

6/10

Location: Cowabunga Bay (Las Vegas)

Opened: 2014

Built by: White Water West

Last Ridden: June 5, 2017
