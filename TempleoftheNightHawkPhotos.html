Crazy Bats Review

We're here at Phantasialand. Today's coaster is actually inside. Yep. We're reviewing Crazy Bats. We get in the cars, pull down the lap bar, and away we go. We head in the dark, and head up a lifthill. We eventually drop down and do a bunch of stuff in the dark. It's not that intense, but it's still fun none the less. We're just roaming around in the dark, just cruising and chilling. Go through another lifthill. And again, cruise, chill, relax. Enjoy the ride. And again, another lifthill, cruise, chill, relax, brake run. And that's Crazy Bats. Sorry that this review is so vague. It's mostly just that...I have no idea what this ride does. All I know is that this coaster is super long, doesn't really do much, meanders in the dark, and lasts for like 10 minutes. Honestly, the only reason this ride is worth riding is because it's a credit. So if you're not a credit whore, feel free to skip this ride.

5/10

Location: Phantasialand

Opened: 1988

Built by: Vekoma

Last Ridden: June 29, 2012
