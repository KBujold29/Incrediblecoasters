Rabalder Review

We're here at Liseberg. And we're reviewing Rabalder. Their kiddy coaster. Meh, it's one of the better kiddy coasters. You climb into the cars, and pull down the lap bar. And we're off!!! We start to climb the lifthill. We reach the top and head down the curved first drop. We head up another curved hill, down another curved drop, and are just sort of coasting and curving. Down another curved drop and through another curved turn. We head up a small hill and glide right into the brake run, before turning around and heading back into the station. Actually, by kiddy coaster standards, this isn't a bad one. It kind of reminds me of a Roller Skater. But sadly, this is NOT the Balder of Kiddy Coasters, even if it's one of the better ones. I don't really recommend kiddy coasters as they're mainly for credit whoring. But this is one of the better ones. But it also gets lines. So yeah. Wouldn't really recommend it.

4/10

Location: Liseberg

Opened: 2009

Built by: Zeirer

Last Ridden: June 22, 2014
