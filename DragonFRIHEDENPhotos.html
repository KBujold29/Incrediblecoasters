Dragen Review

Once you get in the seat, the ride operator pulls you out of the station and away you go. There's a small drop out of the station, you head around a turn, which is followed by the lift hill. After climbing up the 10 ft lifthill, you go around a turn and go down a small drop. Wee. Rise up a little bit, go around a turn, and then down another drop. Around a turn, and CHA-CHING!!! Got the credit. Just ride if you're a credit whore or a child.

2/10

Location: Tivoli Friheden

Opened: 2001

Built by: Pinfari

Last Ridden: June 19, 2014
