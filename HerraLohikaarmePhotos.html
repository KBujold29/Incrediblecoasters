Herra Lohikäärme Review

We're here at Fun Park, where we'll be reviewing their kiddy coaster. Cause admit it. That's the only reason we're here. So let's climb in the car, and off we go. We go around a spiral lifthill. It's only 10 ft. Pathetic. We can jump from this height onto the floor. Hell, I've done that before. And then, a spiral drop. It's only 10 feet. But whatever. Wee. We head around a turn. No, there are no laterals here. And then we get at least 6 more laps on this thing. Dammit!! I'm really thinking of jumping at this point. *Sigh* Well, at least I got my credit. Cha-Ching.

2/10

Location: Fun Park

Opened: 2012

Built by: SBF Visa Group

Last Ridden: June 25, 2014
