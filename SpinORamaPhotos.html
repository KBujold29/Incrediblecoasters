Spin O'Rama Review

We're here at Cliff's Amusement Park, where we'll be reviewing their kiddy coaster. It's a kiddy spinning coaster. Yipee. So let's climb in the car, and off we go. We go around a spiral lifthill. It's only 10 ft. Sad. And then, a spiral drop. It's only 10 feet. But whatever. Wee. We head around a turn and go again. I know this is just a crappy kiddy coaster, but it actually spun decently. Better than some actual adult spinning coasters. That is just pathetic. And then we get at least 6 more laps on this thing. Dammit!! *Sigh* Honestly, if they took a kiddy coaster with one of the bigger layouts, such as the large Vekoma Roller Skaters, and put on these spinning cars, it could actually be a legitimatly fun kiddy coaster. Well, at least I got my credit. Cha-Ching.

3/10

Location: Cliff's Amusement Park

Opened: 2016

Built by: SBF Visa Group

Last Ridden: June 9, 2022
