    East Coast Trip 2021 - Columbus Zoo & Waldameer

    The East Coast Trip us to two new places we haven't been before. The Columbus Zoo & Waldameer. And luckily, both of these places are a ton of fun. But also see the other fun coasters, Skyline Chilli, and other fun at the park.
