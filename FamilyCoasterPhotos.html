Family Coaster Review

Once you get in the seat, you out of the station and away you go. There's a small drop out of the station which is followed by the lift hill. After climbing up the 10 ft lifthill, you go around a turn and is followed by a little dip, the you go through another turn to face the final 6 ft drop then you go around another turn, and then you again. You get 3 laps around the track. It's just your typical average Wacky Worm. You've ridden one, you've ridden a million of them. I would only recommend it if you are a credit whore or are just getting into coasters.

2/10
