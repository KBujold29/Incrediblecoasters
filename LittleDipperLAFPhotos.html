Little Dipper Review

All right. We're here at the L.A County Fair, where we're going to review the smallest coaster here. The Little Dipper. This thing is barely even a credit. You just get in the seats (which are surprisingly pretty big for such a small coaster) and we're off. You go up a tiny lifthill (which is like 15 ft tall) before dropping. Wee. Over a tiny bump. Wow. Around a turn and go again and again and again because all the little kids wanna keep riding even though it only took one lap to get the credit, so now you must show off your shame as you keep going around and around. *Sigh* Oh well. I got the credit. Cha-Ching.

1/10

Location: American Fairs
