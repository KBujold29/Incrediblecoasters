Flashback Review

We're here at Six Flags New England to ride your Typical Boomerang. After climbing in the seat and pulling down the shoulder harness, you dispatch. Up the lift you go backwards. You get a pretty view of the park while going up the lift hill. On this Boomerang, once you get to the top, you drop. The drop is really fun and you get some nice speed. However, once you pull up into the sky as you enter the cobra roll, That's where the pain begins. After you slam into the restraint coming out of the 1st half of the Cobra Roll, you get slammed going into the 2nd half of the cobra roll. Then you go into a loop. The loop is all right and much better than the cobra roll, but it's still not my. Then you climb up the second spike, as we climb up the second spike, you get a chance to recover, but then, you drop. Backwards you go. The loop is all right backwards, but its still not as good as other Boomerangs. And then you go backwards into the cobra roll. It really confuses you as it slams you backwards. And finally, after all the slamming is over, You glide into the station. Personally, I think this wins the award for the most useless Boomerang of all time. Why? Because they ALREADY HAVE A MUCH BETTER BOOMERANG IN THE PARK!!!! Ever since my last visit, the park got DejaVu from Six Flags Magic Mountain, which is essentially the same ride, only much better. Sell this ride for scrap metal and make something useful out of it, or at least send it to some random park in the Middle East where it'd actually thrive. But considering how this is the 3rd park this Boomerangs been to, it would've had a well and successful life if we just demolished it now. So just ride DejaVu (Goliath) and ignore this ride unless you're a credit whore.

6/10
