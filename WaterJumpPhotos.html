Water Jump Review

We're on the Water Jump. It may be a lame name for a water slide, but DAMN! This is certainly one of the more interesting water slides. So let's head down this thing. You slide down and gain A LOT of speed. Seriously, I think this may very well be one of the steepest drops on any water slide. We level out and head up a big ramp and just FLY OFF!!! OH SH*T!!! *SPLASH* And yep. That's it. It's almost like a ski jump, only this thing is a water slide. The closest thing I can compare this water slide to is the now defunct Bombay Blasters back when it was at Wild Rivers (F*CK YOU IRVINE COMP!!!). So that makes me especially glad that this slide exists and I certainly hope that there are more slides like it out there. =)

9/10
