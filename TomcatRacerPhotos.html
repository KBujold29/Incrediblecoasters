Tomcat Racer Review

For this review, we're at New Wild Rivers, here to review their Helix Mat Racer. These things have been around for a while, but really intrigued me just because they look so damn fun. Finally got to try many of these and....they're really fun. We reach the top, and we're off. We give ourselves a good push as we head down a helix drop. You can't see how you're doing compared to the others. But THE FASTER, THE BETTER!!!! Plus, it's more fun anyway to go fast. We then pop out and drop down a couple bumps, turning into a standard Mat Racer. Hopefully we're in first place. And we slide. All in all, this is just a really fun ride that I highly recommend while at New Wild Rivers. Yeah. This isn't nearly as quirky and awkward as Surf Hill, but it's still a lot of fun.

7/10

Location: New Wild Rivers

Opened: 2022

Built by: ProSlide

Last Ridden: September 3, 2022
